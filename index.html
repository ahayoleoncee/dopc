About Us

DOPC Rwanda is a technology services organization based in Rwanda. Founded in 2026 by two students from the Network and Internet Technology (NIT) program at Kivu Hills Academy, DOPC is dedicated to delivering innovative software systems, automation solutions, and digital technologies. Our services include software development, system integration, smart automation solutions such as smart doors and access control systems, IT consulting, and digital transformation services. The organization's mission is to adapt and implement modern technologies within Rwanda at affordable costs, helping businesses, institutions, and citizens benefit from advanced solutions while contributing to the country's economic growth and technological development.
